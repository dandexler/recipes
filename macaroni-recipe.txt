Ingredients:
     2 cups uncooked macaroni noodles
     5 slices American cheese
     1 cup sliced Gouda cheese
     1/2 cup 2% milk
     2 tbsp crushed red pepper
     2 tbsp garlic powder

Directions:
     1) Boil water on stove on maximum heat
     2) Pour in uncooked macaroni noodles and set timer for 8 minutes
     3) Turn heat down to medium after pouring noodles
     4) Strain noodles over sink
     5) Add American cheese, Gouda cheese, and 2% milk to noodles and stir over medium-high heat until fully melted
     6) Stir in crushed red pepper and garlic powder
     7) Serve
